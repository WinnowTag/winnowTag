Contract position: Developer for social applications in Ruby

We’re creating tools to dramatically increase the scope and power of online interaction. We are writing open source web components to both enhance existing services and make new services possible. We need an experienced independent contractor who can make a strong and well defined commitment to our projects. Compensation is competitive.

You must be passionate about the social potential of the web and expert in current open source web techniques. We use Ruby + Rails. A current Ruby developer is preferred. Current work in Python, Smalltalk or Lisp is sufficient if you demonstrate an intense interest in Ruby. Development is web coordinated; we have no office. Hours are flexible but you must make a clearly scheduled and substantial commitment to our projects.

Give us evidence that you:

  • Manage your own time and development focus.
  • Have a practiced habit of clear and efficient communication.
  • Analyze development problems and design clean scaleable solutions.
  • Quickly learn and integrate new technologies making development more effective.
  • Work well with a small team in a web coordinated development process.

Recent graduates are welcome — we hire on merit, not seniority. Please email your resume to j200601b@peerworks.org. Please be sure to direct us to current examples of your work. If there’s a possible match, we’ll contact you.
